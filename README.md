**We are a next-generation CCTV manufacturing and software enterprise, bridging the gap between advanced hardware engineering, edge AI, and seamless web-based surveillance management.**

At **The Thirty Three Company**, we believe the future of security lies in intelligent, autonomous systems. We don't just build cameras; we build end-to-end security ecosystems that manage data securely from the silicon level all the way up to the cloud.

###

<h3 align="left">🚀 What We Do</h3>

Our engineering efforts are split across two core domains to provide a frictionless security experience:

* **🧠 AI-Enabled Embedded Hardware:** We design and manufacture our own line of advanced CCTV cameras. Our embedded software engineering team writes highly optimized, low-level firmware that enables deep learning models (like object detection and facial recognition) to run directly on the edge, reducing latency and bandwidth.
* **☁️ Web-Based Video Management System (VMS):** We build a robust, scalable web application that serves as the central nervous system for our hardware. This VMS allows users to securely monitor live feeds, configure camera settings, manage authentication, and analyze recorded video from anywhere in the world, straight from their browser.

###

<h3 align="left">🌟 Our Vision</h3>

* **Innovation at the Edge:** Pushing the boundaries of what local hardware can compute without relying on heavy cloud processing.
* **Seamless Accessibility:** Making enterprise-grade security accessible through intuitive, modern web interfaces.
* **Uncompromising Security:** Ensuring that both our physical devices and our web backends are fortified against modern vulnerabilities.
